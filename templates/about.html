This site joins "Presidential tweets" and the official events during which they were sent.

  • Conflicts shows tweets that coincided with official meetings
  • Dates is the full list of conflicting dates, each showing a timeline of tweets and events for that date

Sources:

  • Events come from whitehouse.gov/1600daily and archived versions from archive.org.
  • Tweets come from Twitter.

We take the following liberties (I think the point still gets across):

  • When the whitehouse publishes schedules they don't give meeting times, hence no end times. So, the end time of an event is assumed to be the start time of the next event minus 5 minutes.
  • The last event of the day is synthetically and conservatively given a time of one hour, since there is no following event. This is usually an underestimate, since this event often involves travel (e.g. to an out-of-state golf club or Dave and Busters) and takes > 1 hour.
If you don't buy these assumptions, conflicting tweets can just be seen as those sent from the President in the middle of a week day.
